- Added bullet settings
    - Bullet image
    - Bullet speed (how fast it moves)
    - Fire rate (how fast the gun shoots)
    - Lifetime (how long it lasts before it disappear)
    - Bullet spread (determines the accuracy of the gun)

- Added bullet class
    - spawns when the player presses space
    - disappears when hits wall, zombie or exceeded lifetime

Bullets have an offset so that it looks like it is coming out of the gun and not the player's heart
They rotate with the player
